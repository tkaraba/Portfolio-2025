Scope

Design a promotional video as part of the branding material for Znüni, a music mobile application concept that is breaking into the market offering spatial sound

The goal was to create a modern, sleek, and vibrant visual style that highlights the app's UI elements while reflecting a youthful, energetic brand personality.

Process

Brand Direction & Visual Concept

  • Created UI animations to showcase app functionality
